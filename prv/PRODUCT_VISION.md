## 1. WHAT PRV IS

**PRV is a multi-company enterprise ecosystem designed to operate an entire business from a single platform.**

PRV is **not**:
- A website
- A CRM
- A shop
- A project manager
- A collection of separate applications

PRV **is**:
- A **Company Operating System**
- A single ecosystem where every module is connected
- A platform where every role sees a purpose-built experience
- An enterprise system that scales from 1 company to 100+

> The application adapts to the user. The user does not adapt to the application.

## 3. PLATFORM SUPPORT

PRV runs on all major platforms. Apple remains the **premium experience**.

| Platform | Experience Level | Notes |
|----------|----------------|-------|
| **iPhone** | Premium | Primary design target. Full feature set. Dynamic Island. Face ID. |
| **iPad** | Full | 3-panel layout. Keyboard shortcuts. External display support. |
| **Android** | Full | Feature parity with iPhone. Adapted to Material You where native. |
| **Web** | Full | Desktop-first layout. Command Palette primary. Keyboard shortcuts. |
| **macOS** | Full | Native Mac layout. Menu bar integration. Keyboard-first. |

### Design Hierarchy
```
iPhone (designed first)
  ↓ adapted to →
iPad (3-panel, larger surfaces)
  ↓ adapted to →
Web/macOS (full desktop layout)
  ↓ adapted to →
Android (native patterns, feature parity)
```

## 4. CORE PHILOSOPHY

### Apple First
Every interaction, animation, component, and pattern must feel like a first-party Apple product. Users who use iPhone daily should feel immediately at home in PRV.

### Liquid Glass Everywhere
No surface, component, or overlay escapes the Liquid Glass design language. Cards, sheets, menus, modals, notifications, status bars — all glass.

### Role-Based Experience
Every role sees a **different application**:
- A Worker sees their schedule, tasks, and site
- A CEO sees revenue, projects, workforce, and strategic intelligence
- A Cashier sees their register, transactions, and shift
- A Store Manager sees their store's KPIs, staff, and inventory

Same platform. Different experience. Driven entirely by role + scope.

### Mobile First
Designed for iPhone. Optimized for all platforms. No feature exists only on desktop.

### Enterprise Grade
Scales to 100+ companies, 1,000+ stores, 10,000+ employees, millions of records — without redesign. Every decision is made with 10+ years of scale in mind.

## 8. MULTI-COMPANY STRUCTURE

PRV is built for multi-company operation from day one.

```
PRV Group (root)
  │
  ├── PRV Renovations
  │     ├── Divisions
  │     ├── Departments
  │     ├── Teams
  │     └── Employees
  │
  ├── PRV Projects
  │     ├── Project Teams
  │     └── Project Staff
  │
  ├── PRV Shop
  │     ├── Stores
  │     ├── Warehouses
  │     └── Shop Staff
  │
  └── [Future Companies — pluggable]
```

### Visibility Rules

| Role | Can See |
|------|---------|
| CEO / Superadmin | All companies in the group |
| Company Owner | Own company only |
| Regional Manager | Assigned regions only |
| Department Manager | Own department only |
| Team Leader | Own team only |
| Employee | Own records only |

### Company Isolation
- Data between companies is strictly isolated
- Employees of Company A cannot see data of Company B
- Cross-company data only visible to roles with explicit multi-company scope
- Inter-company transactions are formally recorded and audited

### Future Companies
The architecture supports adding new companies to PRV Group without platform changes:
- New company is registered
- Company-specific roles and settings are configured
- Employees are invited
- The platform adapts automatically

## 9. MULTI-STORE STRUCTURE

The Shop platform supports multi-store operations:

```
PRV Shop (company)
  │
  ├── Regions
  │     ├── Region A
  │     │     ├── Store 1
  │     │     └── Store 2
  │     └── Region B
  │           └── Store 3
  │
  └── Warehouses
        ├── Central Warehouse
        └── Regional Warehouse
```

### Store Visibility Rules

| Role | Can See |
|------|---------|
| Shop Director | All stores in the company |
| Regional Manager | All stores in their region |
| Store Manager | Their assigned store only |
| Cashier | Their register / their store |

### Multi-Store Capabilities
- Each store has its own inventory, staff, and KPIs
- Stock transfers between stores and warehouses
- Centralized purchasing with per-store delivery
- Network-level reporting (all stores combined)
- Per-store performance benchmarking

## 10. LONG-TERM VISION — CEO 60-SECOND RULE

**Within 60 seconds of opening PRV, a CEO must know:**

| # | Information | Source |
|---|-------------|--------|
| 1 | **Revenue** | Finance Platform — today / MTD / YTD |
| 2 | **Profit** | Finance Platform — gross and net |
| 3 | **Active Projects** | Project Management Platform — count, health, delays |
| 4 | **Workforce Status** | Attendance Platform — present / absent / on leave |
| 5 | **Inventory Risks** | Shop Platform — low stock, out of stock alerts |
| 6 | **Critical Alerts** | All platforms — P0 issues requiring CEO attention |
| 7 | **AI Recommendations** | AI Platform — top 3 actionable insights |

**Without opening another application.**

### Implementation of the 60-Second Rule
- CEO Cockpit pre-computed server-side every 5 minutes
- Data renders from cache instantly (zero computation at open time)
- Real-time WebSocket updates for live changes
- All 7 data points visible in a single view — no scrolling required
- Every data point tappable for drill-down via Bottom Sheet

### Long-Term Platform Vision

PRV is built for **10+ years of operation**. Every architectural decision must support:

| Horizon | Capability |
|---------|-----------|
| Year 1–2 | Single company, core platforms operational |
| Year 3–5 | Multi-company, full 18-platform ecosystem |
| Year 5–10 | 100+ companies, franchise model, marketplace |
| Year 10+ | PRV Academy, PRV Property, PRV Marketplace, external API |

The platform must never require a full redesign to support growth. The database, permission model, navigation, and component system are all designed to accommodate scale without architectural changes.
