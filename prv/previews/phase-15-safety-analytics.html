What this is: Phase 15 — Safety Analytics (safety-domain BI). A read-only view at /analytics/safety (⌘K: "Safety Analytics"). Aggregates the safety incident log into headline counts (total, open, injuries), a severity-weighted risk index over open incidents (low ×1, medium ×3, high ×7, critical ×15) with a band — critical (red) if any open critical, elevated (amber) if any open high, else stable — plus a severity mix, a type breakdown, resolution rate and mean time to resolve. Read-only aggregation over existing incident data.
