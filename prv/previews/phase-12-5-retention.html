What this is: Phase 12.5 — Document Retention & Expiry Intelligence. Dashboard at /documents/retention (⌘K: "Retention"). A pure helper computes each document's effective expiry (explicit expiry, else created-date + the type's retention months), a band (expired / approaching-30 / approaching-14 / active / on-hold), and auto-archive + GDPR-erasure eligibility. Per-type retention_policies (new table) override the statutory defaults. A legal hold (new columns on documents) exempts a document from all archival and erasure. Red = expired, amber = approaching, per the design system. No file storage touched — pure metadata + workflow.
