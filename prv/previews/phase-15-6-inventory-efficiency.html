What this is: Phase 15.6 — Inventory Efficiency (cross-module BI). A read-only view at /analytics/inventory-efficiency (⌘K: "Inventory Efficiency"). Joins each product's procurement cost (Finance, cost price × on-hand) with its sell-through (Shop, "sale" stock movements over the window) to derive an annualized turnover and days-on-hand. Ranked worst-first by tied-up capital. Bands: dead = stock on hand with zero sales (red — frozen capital), slow = <2 turns/yr (amber), healthy = 2–6, fast = ≥6. Read-only aggregation over existing stock-movement + product data.
