What this is: Phase 15 — Attendance Analytics (workforce-domain BI). A read-only view at /analytics/attendance (⌘K: "Attendance Analytics"). Aggregates the attendance record log into attendance rate (days worked ÷ days scheduled, excluding approved leave), punctuality (on-time ÷ showed-up), absenteeism, and average lateness, plus a status mix and a per-employee watchlist — anyone under 95% attendance, worst first: watch 85–95% (amber), poor <85% (red). Read-only aggregation over existing attendance data.
