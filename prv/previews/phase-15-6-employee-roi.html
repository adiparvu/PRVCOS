What this is: Phase 15.6 — Employee ROI (cross-module BI). A read-only view at /analytics/employee-roi (⌘K: "Employee ROI"). Joins each employee's payroll cost (HR, gross paid) with their completed project tasks (Projects) to derive a cost-per-task — a proxy for delivery per euro — ranked cheapest first. Bands compare each person to the team average: high ≤ 75% of average cost/task, low ≥ 125% (amber), else steady; no output flags anyone paid with zero completed tasks (amber). Read-only aggregation over existing payroll + task data.
