What this is: Phase 21 — Supplier Spend & Payables. A read-only board at /analytics/supplier-spend (⌘K: "Supplier Spend"). Rolls the supplier-invoice ledger up per supplier into committed spend (invoiced, excluding cancelled), the unpaid outstanding balance, and overdue payables (unpaid past due date), ranked by spend with overdue amounts flagged red. On-time payment metrics are omitted — the payable schema has no paid-at timestamp, so a computed on-time rate would be guessed, not measured.
