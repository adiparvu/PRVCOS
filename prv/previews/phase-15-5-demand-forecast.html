What this is: Phase 15.5 — Inventory Demand Forecast. A read-only view at /analytics/demand-forecast (⌘K: "Demand Forecast"). Estimates each product's daily velocity from the trailing 90-day sale movements, then projects days of cover and expected demand over a 30-day horizon. Plans: critical (red) — at/below reorder point or <7 days cover; reorder (amber) — runs out within the horizon; overstock — >180 days cover or no sales; else healthy. Suggested order qty restores cover to the horizon plus the reorder buffer. Read-only aggregation over existing stock data.
