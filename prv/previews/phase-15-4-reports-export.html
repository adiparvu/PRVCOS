What this is: Phase 15.4 — Reports / CSV export. A read-only hub at /analytics/reports (⌘K: "Reports"). Each card downloads the current live dataset from its analytics endpoint and serializes it to a CSV file (RFC-4180 quoting, Excel-friendly) entirely in the browser — no new server surface. Satisfies the Phase 15 exit criterion "create and export a custom report." The white download button is the one inverted-for-CTA accent permitted by the design system; everything else stays monochrome glass.
