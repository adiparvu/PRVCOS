What this is: Phase 13.4 — Announcement Read-Receipts & Acknowledgment. Dashboard at /communications/announcements/receipts (⌘K: "Announcement Receipts"). Announcements now carry a priority (Info / Important / Critical), an optional acknowledgment-required flag and an expiry. A pure helper computes read % and ack % against the audience and the outstanding unread/unacked counts, plus a lifecycle (scheduled / active / expired). A new announcement_acknowledgments table + POST .../[id]/acknowledge records an explicit acknowledgment (idempotent), distinct from a passive read. Amber = important, red = critical, per the design system.
