What this is: Phase 21 — Fleet Readiness. A read-only board at /analytics/fleet-readiness (⌘K: "Fleet Readiness"). Each operating vehicle (active or in maintenance) is ready, needs attention (amber), or is grounded (red) based on service intervals (mileage vs the next-service odometer), insurance/ITP expiry, maintenance status, and fuel level — with a fleet readiness rate and a reasoned attention list, grounded first. Retired/sold vehicles are excluded from the operating fleet.
