What this is: Phase 18.4 — Safety Metrics dashboard. A read-only board at /analytics/safety-metrics (⌘K: "Safety Metrics"). Officer KPIs derived from the incident log: the prominent days-since-last-incident streak (near-misses and hazards don't reset it), incidents in the last 30 days, the recordable vs near-miss split with the near-miss ratio (higher = healthier reporting), and high-risk locations by recordable incident density. TRIR/LTIR are intentionally left out — they require hours-worked data the platform doesn't yet capture, and a fabricated rate would mislead.
