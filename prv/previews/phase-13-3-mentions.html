What this is: Phase 13.3 — Mentions Log. A standalone view at /communications/mentions (⌘K: "Mentions"). Aggregates every channel message where you're in mentionedUserIds (already written on each message), newest first, with author + channel + a snippet and the @you highlighted. A pure helper parses @handles from text, resolves them against the member directory, and buckets your mentions into total / today / this-week. Read-only, company-scoped — no new writes, no changes to existing message routes.
