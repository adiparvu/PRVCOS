What this is: Phase 21 — Fleet Utilization. A read-only board at /analytics/fleet-utilization (⌘K: "Fleet Utilization"). From the last 30 days of daily odometer logs it derives km driven per vehicle (latest − earliest reading) and the days each was logged, plus fleet totals — total distance, active vehicles, average per vehicle and per day — with a top-movers ranking. Monochrome; a vehicle needs two readings to show distance.
