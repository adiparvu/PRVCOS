What this is: Phase 11.5 — Financial Forecasts. Read-only dashboard at /finance/forecast (⌘K: "Forecast"). A pure helper projects a forward P&L from three live inputs — the trailing revenue run-rate (paid invoices), the weighted sales pipeline (reusing the CRM stage-probability model, spread across its first months), and the expense run-rate (approved/paid expenses) — producing per-month revenue/expense/net, a break-even month, and conservative / base / optimistic scenarios over 3 / 6 / 12 months. No schema changes, no writes — pure aggregation of existing finance + CRM data.
