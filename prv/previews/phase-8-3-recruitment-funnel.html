What this is: Phase 8.3 — Recruitment Funnel. A read-only board at /analytics/recruitment-funnel (⌘K: "Recruitment Funnel"). Because each candidate carries its current stage, "reached" a stage means at that stage or beyond — so the counts descend into a proper funnel with the stage-to-stage conversion shown per row, plus active/hired/rejected totals, the overall conversion (hired ÷ entered) and a by-source breakdown. Rejected candidates are excluded from the funnel but reported. Monochrome throughout.
