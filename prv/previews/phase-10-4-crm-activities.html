What this is: Phase 10.4 — CRM Activities. A standalone timeline at /crm/activities (⌘K: "Activities"), plus a new crm_activities table. Log or schedule calls, emails, meetings, demos, proposals, follow-ups, notes and tasks against any lead or customer. Activities derive three states — overdue (past due, not done, flagged amber), scheduled, and done (with a recorded outcome). The summary strip counts open / overdue / done. Amber is used strictly as an overdue status signal, per the design system.
